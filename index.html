Public variables

matherial, type, enviroment, pilot, ammo, volume, speed, affiliation, weapon, health - is public variables in constructor and it can be redefined when we need.

Private variables and methods

Galaxy has one standard of technical controll so functions which validate spaceship by it's current characteristics is private and can not be modified outside.

point is local variable and visible just inside constructor. quolityControll - is private method and it has no access outside constructor.

* JavaScript can't make method or property protected that is why fake can be created. Ship with fake quolityControll method automaticaly become out of law.
